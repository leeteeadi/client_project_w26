Do you need tools to assist you in effective networking? You can meet with a CDO Career Coach to review your materials.

Networking Strategies

  • Attend Events: Participate in industry meetups, conferences, and UMSI networking events to connect with professionals.
  • Leverage LinkedIn: Create a strong LinkedIn profile, join relevant groups, and engage with content to expand your network.
  • Informational Interviews: Reach out to professionals for informational interviews to learn about their career paths and gain insights.
  • Follow Up: After meeting someone, send a personalized follow-up message to express your appreciation and maintain the connection.
  • Be Genuine: Focus on building authentic relationships rather than just collecting contacts.
